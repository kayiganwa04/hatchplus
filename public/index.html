Welcome to Hatchplus

Hatchplus is a company that manufactures and distributes incubators that optimize the hatching of poultry eggs at a maximum rate. Our smart egg incubator promotes accurate controls for temperature and humidity keeping fluctuations at the minimum level for undisputed top efficiency.

Hatchplus is providing huge solution to the community in terms of products and services

We created a fully automated, solar powered hatching machine which drastically hatches chicks easily and affordably at farmhouse level and substantially saving at least 75% of the cost of chicks. Having the hatch rate of more than 90% which is the most competitive globally and producing up to 30,000 chicks per station within 21days only. To satisfy the demand, our innovative flexible business model will enable small and medium holders to acquire affordable and enough DOCs supplied from our decentralized hatching stations without buying an expensive incubator but enable them to use it as service. Our vision is that at least 1,248,000 Households countrywide will own at least 10 chicks monthly to ensure food security.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What impact are you generating in your country/countries of operation.

    Our stations are built from 30% of recycled non decomposing materials which are refurbished and fine-tuned to fit desired performance (power supply unit from old pcs, power cables, capacitors, resistors,...). it reduces reduce greenhouse gas emissions and production cost as well.

  • What key performance indicators (KPIs) do you use, or intend to use, to measure the social and environmental impact of your business?

    we have a recycling aspiration of at least 50% of our station ingredients by gradually acquiring 5% each year. 2. cut our production and operation energy bill 60% from non renewable to renewable sources.

  • What is your your revenue generation model?

    Our innovative decentralized station model will position our revenue streams to rise drastically from economies of scale and establish our pricing scheme as the most competitive price on the market with 40% lower than others.

  • How do you know people need your solution?

    During product development process, we conducted pilot project to acquire demand validation and product market fit. 5 incubators have been given to 5 cooperatives of northern province. findings indicated a huge demand and there are even some customers who are investing substitutes which don't satisfy their needs.

  • What problem are you solving and for whom?

    Africa has more than 15 million poultry farms, and the local production is less than 40% of the market demand. Just in 2018, African imports stood at US1.9 billion, an increase of 23% over 2017. This shortage of local production of chicken is derived from Small and medium holders who are the majority players in the industry and are struggling with expensive and insufficient supply of DOC (Day Old Chicks). The demand for day old chicks is strong, and local hatcheries are unable to provide it. The majority of day-old chicks are imported from Brazil, china and Europe.
